## üìö PART 1: PEDAGOGICAL FOUNDATION

### **Core Educational Principles**

#### **1. Zone of Proximal Development (Vygotsky)**
- System adapts to student's current level
- Provides scaffolding just beyond their ability
- Gradually removes support as mastery develops

#### **2. Constructivist Learning**
- Students create knowledge through mythologies
- Real-world authentic tasks
- Social learning through collaboration features

#### **3. Differentiated Instruction**
- Multiple pathways to learning
- Varied assignment types
- Support for diverse learning styles

#### **4. Standards-Based Education**
- Align activities with educational standards
- Track mastery of specific skills
- Evidence-based assessment
