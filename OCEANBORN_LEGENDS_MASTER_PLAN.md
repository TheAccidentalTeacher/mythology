### **PHASE 1: Divine Hierarchy** (Supreme Powers)
*Goal: Establish the core pantheon structure*

#### Tier 1: Primordial Forces (3)
1. **Abyssara** - Primordial Ocean, The Deep Mother
   - Type: Primordial deity (formless)
   - Domain: The infinite ocean itself, source of all water
   - Powers: Creation, destruction, tides
   - Personality: Ancient, unknowable, rarely intervenes
   
2. **Tideus** - The First Current
   - Type: Primordial force
   - Domain: Movement, change, flow of time
   - Powers: Controls all currents, can speed/slow time
   - Personality: Chaotic neutral, embodies constant change

3. **Pressura** - The Crushing Depth
   - Type: Primordial entity
   - Domain: Weight, darkness, fear
   - Powers: Can manifest immense pressure, controls darkness
   - Personality: Cold, impassive, judges all who descend

#### Tier 2: Supreme Gods (5)
4. **Nautrion** - King of the Deep, The Coral Throne
   - Type: God (male)
   - Domain: Sovereignty, deep-sea kingdoms, coral reefs
   - Appearance: Muscular, 10-foot tall, coral crown, glowing blue eyes, trident of living coral
   - Powers: Command sea creatures, earthquake generation, water manipulation
   - Personality: Proud, just but harsh, expects absolute loyalty
   - Conflicts: Constantly challenged by his siblings

5. **Marineth** - Queen of Tides, The Moon's Daughter
   - Type: Goddess (female)
   - Domain: Tides, navigation, prophecy
   - Appearance: Ethereal, flowing silver-blue hair, eyes like pearls, robes of foam
   - Powers: Control tides, see future, guide sailors
   - Personality: Compassionate but aloof, speaks in riddles
   - Relationship: Wife of Nautrion (political marriage)

6. **Abyssor** - Lord of the Trenches, The Devourer Below
   - Type: God (male)
   - Domain: Deep trenches, darkness, secrets
   - Appearance: Shadowy, angler-fish features, bioluminescent lures, massive teeth
   - Powers: Control abyssal creatures, induce madness, reveal hidden truths
   - Personality: Cunning, patient, hoards secrets
   - Conflicts: Nautrion's rebellious brother, wants the throne

7. **Luminara** - Lady of Bioluminescence, The Glowing One
   - Type: Goddess (female)
   - Domain: Light in darkness, hope, beauty
   - Appearance: Translucent, jellyfish-like trailing tendrils, glows with inner light
   - Powers: Create light, inspire hope, charm creatures
   - Personality: Optimistic, naive, believes in redemption
   - Relationship: Sister to Nautrion, mediator in conflicts

8. **Krakus** - The Storm Bringer, Wrath of the Surface
   - Type: God (male)
   - Domain: Storms, waves, destruction
   - Appearance: Massive, tentacled arms, whirlpool eyes, voice like thunder
   - Powers: Summon storms, create whirlpools, lightning strikes
   - Personality: Wrathful, impulsive, easily offended
   - Conflicts: Despises mortals who sail his waters
