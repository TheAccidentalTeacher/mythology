**Age-appropriate content guidelines for Mythology Builder Platform (Grades 6-8)**

---

## üéØ POLICY OVERVIEW

Mythology Builder is designed for middle school students (ages 11-14) to explore mythology and creative writing in a **safe, respectful, and educational** environment. This policy defines what content is allowed, what is prohibited, and how moderation works.

---

## ‚úÖ ALLOWED CONTENT

### **Mythology-Appropriate Violence**

Middle school mythology studies include battles, monsters, and character deaths. These are educational and age-appropriate:

**‚úÖ PERMITTED:**
- **Mythological battles:** Gods fighting titans, heroes defeating monsters
- **Character deaths:** "The hero fell in battle," "Medusa was slain by Perseus"
- **Fantasy weapons:** Swords, lightning bolts, magical staffs, tridents
- **Monster abilities:** "The dragon breathed fire," "Cerberus guards the underworld"
- **Transformation/punishment:** "Zeus turned them to stone," "Athena cursed Medusa"
- **Underworld/afterlife:** Hades, Valhalla, Egyptian Duat (mythology-accurate)

**Examples of ACCEPTABLE descriptions:**
- "Zeus hurled his lightning bolt at the titan"
- "The hero drew his sword and faced the creature"
- "Medusa's gaze turned warriors to stone"
- "The battle raged for three days until the god emerged victorious"

---

### **Fantasy Creatures & Powers**

Students create gods, monsters, and magical beings with supernatural abilities:

**‚úÖ PERMITTED:**
- **Elemental powers:** Fire, water, lightning, wind, earth
- **Shape-shifting:** Gods transforming into animals or humans
- **Immortality:** Gods cannot die (mythology-accurate)
- **Magical abilities:** Teleportation, flying, super strength, prophecy
- **Creature features:** Wings, horns, multiple heads, scales, claws

---

### **Cultural & Historical Accuracy**

Students study real mythologies from various cultures:

**‚úÖ PERMITTED:**
- Accurate depictions of Greek, Norse, Egyptian, Roman mythologies
- Respectful references to religious/cultural traditions
- Comparative mythology (Zeus = Jupiter, Odin = Wotan)
- Age-appropriate discussion of ancient cultural practices

---

## ‚ùå PROHIBITED CONTENT

### **Category 1: Graphic Violence**

While mythology includes battles, descriptions must be age-appropriate.

**‚ùå NOT ALLOWED:**
- **Graphic gore:** Detailed descriptions of blood, dismemberment, torture
- **Gratuitous violence:** Violence for shock value (not mythologically relevant)
- **Real-world violence:** School shootings, terrorism, war crimes
- **Animal cruelty:** Detailed descriptions of harming animals
- **Self-harm:** Cutting, suicide, eating disorders

**Examples of INAPPROPRIATE violence:**
- ‚ùå "Blood spurted from the wound as he screamed in agony"
- ‚ùå "The god slowly tortured the mortal for hours"
- ‚ùå "He cut himself with the blade"

**Corrected versions:**
- ‚úÖ "The hero fell in battle"
- ‚úÖ "The god punished the mortal by turning him to stone"
- ‚úÖ "The battle was fierce but ended quickly"

---

### **Category 2: Sexual Content**

No sexual content is permitted for middle school students.

**‚ùå NOT ALLOWED:**
- Sexual acts or descriptions
- Nudity or descriptions of naked bodies
- Romantic relationships beyond age-appropriate (hand-holding, crushes OK)
- Sexualized character appearances
- Inappropriate relationships (adult-minor, incest beyond mythology references)

**‚ö†Ô∏è Special Case: Greek Mythology Relationships**
- Greek mythology includes complex family trees (Zeus-Hera are siblings)
- **Allowed:** Factual statement "Zeus married his sister Hera" (historical accuracy)
- **Not Allowed:** Romanticizing or detailed descriptions of such relationships

---

### **Category 3: Hate Speech & Discrimination**

Mythology Builder celebrates diversity and inclusivity.

**‚ùå NOT ALLOWED:**
- Racial slurs or stereotypes
- Religious bigotry
- Homophobic/transphobic language
- Sexist language
- Disability-based insults
- Ethnic stereotyping

**Cultural Sensitivity:**
- Students may create characters from various cultural backgrounds
- Depictions must be respectful (avoid stereotypes)
- When referencing real mythologies (African, Asian, Native American), research accurately

---

### **Category 4: Bullying & Harassment**

**‚ùå NOT ALLOWED:**
- Personal attacks on classmates
- Using platform to harass others
- Naming characters after classmates to mock them
- Revealing personal information about others
- Exclusionary behavior (in collaboration features)

---

### **Category 5: Inappropriate Language**

**‚ùå NOT ALLOWED:**
- Profanity: F-word, S-word, etc.
- Sexual language
- Slurs targeting any group

**‚úÖ MYTHOLOGY-APPROPRIATE LANGUAGE:**
- "Damn" in mythology context ("The gods damned the mortal to the underworld") - **Allowed**
- Creative alternatives: "Blast!" "Curses!" "By the gods!"

---

### **Category 6: Dangerous Activities**

**‚ùå NOT ALLOWED:**
- Instructions for weapons/explosives
- Drug/alcohol use or glorification
- Illegal activities
- Dangerous challenges

## ü§ñ AUTOMATED MODERATION

### **How It Works:**

1. **Student submits content** (character, story, description)
2. **AI scans content** using OpenAI Moderation API
3. **Analysis for:**
   - Hate speech
   - Violence (excessive)
   - Sexual content
   - Self-harm
   - Profanity

4. **Result:**
   - **‚úÖ Pass:** Content saved, published
   - **‚ùå Fail:** Content rejected, student sees error

**Student sees:** *"Your content violates community guidelines. Please revise and try again."*

**Teacher sees:** Flagged content in "Flagged Content" queue with reason

---

### **False Positives:**

AI moderation sometimes over-flags mythology-appropriate content.

**Example:**
- Student writes: "The god of death reigned over the underworld"
- AI flags: "violence/death-related"
- **Solution:** Teacher manually reviews and approves

**For Teachers:**
- Review flagged content in **"Flagged Content"** tab
- Approve if mythology-appropriate
- Reject if genuinely inappropriate
- Provide feedback to student

---

## üîç MANUAL REVIEW PROCESS

### **Tiered Review System:**

#### **Tier 1: Automated (Instant)**
- All content scanned by AI
- Clear violations automatically rejected
- No human needed for obvious cases

#### **Tier 2: Teacher Review (24-48 hours)**
- Borderline content flagged for teacher
- Teacher approves or rejects
- Feedback provided to student

#### **Tier 3: Escalation (Serious Violations)**
- Threats, bullying, hate speech ‚Üí Escalate to admin
- Contact parents if needed
- Potential account suspension

## üö® ENFORCEMENT & CONSEQUENCES

### **First Offense (Minor):**
- Content rejected
- Guidance provided: "Please revise to be age-appropriate"
- No penalty

### **Second Offense (Pattern):**
- Teacher notified
- Student receives warning message
- Teacher reviews student's account

### **Third Offense (Repeated Violations):**
- Teacher contacts parents
- Temporary content creation suspension (3-7 days)
- Meeting with student about guidelines

### **Serious Violations (Threats, Bullying, Hate Speech):**
- Immediate account suspension
- Parents contacted same day
- Admin/principal notified
- Potential permanent ban
