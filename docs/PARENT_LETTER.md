### **What Parents Should Know About Mythology Content**

**"Will my child be exposed to inappropriate content?"**

No. Mythology studies include battles and character deaths (Zeus defeats Titans, heroes slay monsters), but we keep content age-appropriate:

‚úÖ **Allowed:** "The hero defeated the monster in battle"  
‚ùå **Not Allowed:** Graphic violence, gore, or disturbing descriptions

All content is moderated before other students can see it.

---

**"What if my child sees another student's inappropriate content?"**

- All content is reviewed by me before being shared
- Students can report content with a "Report" button
- I receive immediate notification and review within 24 hours

---

**"Is the AI feature safe?"**

Yes. AI features have safeguards:
- Daily limits prevent over-reliance (10 images, 20 text assists per day)
- AI-generated images are scanned for inappropriate content
- AI writing assistance provides *suggestions*, not complete work
- I review all submissions to ensure student originality

---

**"What about screen time?"**

- Most work is completed during class time
- At-home work is optional (not required)
- Project encourages *productive* screen use (writing, creativity)
- Recommended at-home limit: 30-45 minutes per session, 2-3x per week

---

**"Can I see my child's work?"**

Yes! Two options:
1. Ask your child to log in and show you (encourages discussion)
2. Email me for access or screenshots

---

**"What if my child struggles with creative writing?"**

The platform includes:
- Templates and examples to inspire ideas
- AI assistance for brainstorming (not writing for them)
- My feedback on drafts before final submission
- Peer collaboration opportunities

---

**"Is this replacing traditional writing?"**

No. This project *enhances* writing instruction:
- Students still write narratives (500+ words)
- Focus on character development, plot, and descriptive writing
- Added digital literacy and presentation skills
- Combines traditional ELA with modern tools
